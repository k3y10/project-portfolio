Embarking on a journey to create a personalized Geo Time Scale application for my girlfriend's final college project has been a heartwarming experience, filled with love, creativity, and dedication. This project, lovingly named the Geo Time Scale, is a testament to our shared memories and the special moments we've cherished together.

## Project Overview

The Geo Time Scale is a personalized application designed to chronicle our relationship milestones and travel adventures on an interactive map-based timeline. It provides a visually engaging platform to document and celebrate our journey together, from our first date to our most recent travels.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: Utilized for frontend development, providing server-side rendering and a seamless user experience.
- **React.js**: Powering the dynamic and interactive user interface of the Geo Time Scale.
- **Mapbox API**: Integration of the Mapbox API for creating an interactive map interface with custom markers and layers.
- **Native Pop-ups**: Implemented native pop-ups for seamless interaction and information display when clicking on map markers.
- **Full PWA**: Configured the application as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for enhanced performance and offline accessibility.
- **Vertical Time Scale**: Designed a unique vertical time scale for easy navigation and exploration of our relationship timeline.
- **Navigation Slider**: Implemented a navigation slider for smooth scrolling and browsing through our shared memories and adventures.

## Conclusion

Creating the Geo Time Scale has been a labor of love and creativity, strengthening our bond and allowing us to relive cherished memories together. As we continue to add new chapters to our journey, the Geo Time Scale will serve as a timeless reminder of our love, growth, and adventures together.
